Prologue:

A cable from the Argentine Intelligence Service dated August 21, 1985, reported more than ten temporal disturbances, attributed to a possible extraterrestrial incident in the South Atlantic. Due to these anomalies, the cover-up, and the subsequent historical fragmentation, the authenticity of the events portrayed in this work could not be corroborated.

Teaser Trailer of the Original Series – Subatomika - October 1986.

A coming-of-age historical fiction thriller written and directed by Mars Dostal. Original song: "I Was There..." by Votrox

Step into an immersive #AIfilm series that blends punk culture and sci-fi in the gritty, electric backdrop of 1980s Buenos Aires. This photo-realistic, action-packed mystery follows Veronica and her friends, a group of teens navigating their chaotic punk lifestyle and new freedoms, all while living under the shadow of state oppression. But their rebellion takes a wild turn when they stumble into a time-travel mystery that could rewrite everything they know. Inspired by real events, The Breakfast Club (1985) by John Hughes, and LOST (2004) by J.J. Abrams, Jeffrey Lieber, and Damon Lindelof, this series is a mashup of raw emotion, suspense, and unfiltered nostalgia.
